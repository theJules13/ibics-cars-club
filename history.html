History of IBICS Cars Club

In November 1996, IBICS was founded as the first German-speaking interest group dedicated to the brands Iso and Bizzarrini. Martin Aeschlimann, along with several brand enthusiasts, established this institution at that time.

The popular publication "ibics facts" has since been released in more than 50 editions and remains the only German-language publication regularly covering the Iso & Bizzarrini brands.

IBICS has been present on the internet since 1998 and currently boasts around 60 members, including many from abroad. In 2009, the interest group was transformed into an association.

Over the years, IBICS has organized memorable events for its members and has been instrumental in assisting with numerous restorations of Iso and Bizzarrini vehicles.
